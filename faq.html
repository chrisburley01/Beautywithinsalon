Frequently Asked Questions

How much does wrinkle softening cost?

Wrinkle softening starts from £200 depending on area and consultation. We’ll assess suitability and tailor a plan during your visit.

How long do dermal fillers and wrinkle softening last?

Wrinkle softening typically lasts 3–4 months. Dermal fillers usually last 6–12 months depending on the product and area treated.

What are skin boosters and who are they for?

Skin boosters (e.g., Profhilo®, Seventy Hyal®, Polynucleotides) hydrate and improve skin quality. Great for dullness, fine lines and crepey texture.
