Wrinkle Softening vs Fillers — What’s Right for Me?

Wrinkle softening relaxes movement lines (like frown or forehead) while dermal fillers add gentle volume or contour (lips, cheeks, jawline). Many clients benefit from a blend for balanced, natural results.

  • Softening: 3–4 months typical longevity.
  • Fillers: 6–12 months depending on area/product.

Aftercare Tips: Make Your Results Last

Good aftercare helps results look better for longer. Avoid touching the area on the day, stay hydrated, minimise alcohol for 24 hours, and follow any specific guidance you’re given in clinic.

  • Use SPF daily to protect collagen and pigment.
  • Book reviews as advised to fine-tune outcomes.
